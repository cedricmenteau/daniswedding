Our Story

In April 2009 Eva travelled from Fribourg in western Switzerland to the beautiful island of Pico. Same did Daniel from Freiburg in southwest Germany. In the small town of Madalena one could find only a single holiday appartment, so – you could call it destiny – they had to be together under the same roof from the very beginning. At that magic place in the middle of the atlantic ocean, they climbed portugal’s highest mountain and fell in love while whatching whales, walking over lava fields and enjoying the island life.

Despite all difficulties they have been fighting since then for their love and partnership. They created lots of beautiful moments and shed more than one tear.

In 2012 they felt their love would be great enough for a small wonder in their middle. In fact it happened to be enough for two. Since June 2013 Lina and Hanna fill their lifes with happiness.

In late summer 2015 Eva finally fulfilled Daniel’s dream during brunch at the shore of lake Zurich by handing him a spoon with the engraved words: „I would love to be your wife“.

Important Informations

Dress code

Although the general topic of our wedding is "vintage", there is no actual dresscode. Nevertheless, the gentlemen are invited to wear those suspenders and a bow tie, if they feel like it and the ladies might want to browse through their vintage dresses for the occasion.

The location is situated at an altitude of 942 m and exposed to the wind, so it might get slightly cold in the evening even in july – bring a summer jacket or anything that’ll keep you warm then.

The terrain is not Louboutin-safe. There’s grass, mud, gravel so we recommend comfortable shoes you won’t be too sad if the get stained.

Accommodation

65 bunk beds (bring your own sleeping bag), possibility to set up a tent outside or sleep in one of the rooms on a sleeping mat. We plan on partying until the day, there will be breakfast with view on the lake Zurich, so we invite you warmly to sleep in the cosy dormitory – if you need to. You sleep for free.

For those who prefer the comfort of a real hotel, we could ask for group rates (depending on the number of guests) at the Holiday Inn Schindellegi (+/- 170 CHF/night for a double room) ot h the Ibis in Baar (+/- 100 CHF/night for a double room), please indicate in the RSVP if you whish to stay at the hotel.

Location & Travel information

Ferienheim Mistlibühl, 8825 Hütten, Schweiz
Please indicate your travelling mode in the RSVP, so we can organize shuttles, if needed.

By car

From Zurich -> Take A3 direction Chur -> Highway Exit Richterswil -> follow direction Samstagern/Einsiedeln -> Take second possibility towards Hütten (on the little bridge) -> in the village take a sharp left turn where the balloons are and follow the street through the forest until you see the parking lot.

From Chur/Lindau -> Take A3 direction Zurich -> Highway Exit Schindellegi/Einsiedeln -> take the road uphill-> in Schindellegi keep right (first exit in the roundabout) -> after Schindellegi first possibility towards Hütten to the left (on the little bridge) -> in the village take a sharp left turn where the balloons are and follow the street through the forest until you see the parking lot.

By plane

Nearest airport is Zurich, get a rental car from there or indicate in the RSVP if you need a lift from the nearest train station.

By train

Nearest train station is Schindellegi, we can also pick you up at Wädenswil. Please indicate.
